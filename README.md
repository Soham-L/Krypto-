# 🔐 Krypto

> The crypto wallet that puts security before hype.
>
> Built for a generation that wants complete control of their digital assets without sacrificing simplicity.

---

## Why Krypto?

Crypto was meant to give people freedom.

Instead, most wallets overwhelm users with complicated interfaces, confusing approvals, endless warnings, and security risks that can cost them everything with a single mistake.

Krypto was born from a simple idea:

> What if the safest crypto wallet was also the easiest one to use?

No clutter. No unnecessary complexity.

Just a beautifully designed wallet that helps people make smarter and safer decisions.

---

## ✨ What Makes Krypto Different?

### 🛡️ Security That Actually Protects You
Most wallets simply ask, "Do you approve?"

Krypto asks:

- Is this transaction suspicious?
- Have you interacted with this address before?
- Does this approval grant unlimited access?
- Could this action put your assets at risk?

Because prevention is better than recovery.

---

### ⚡ Designed for Humans

Krypto is built for people, not just crypto experts.

- Clean and intuitive interface
- Guided transaction flows
- Minimal learning curve
- Clear explanations instead of technical jargon
- Fast access to the actions you use most

---

### 🤖 Intelligent Protection

Krypto doesn't just store assets.

It helps protect them.

Future versions will include an AI-powered security assistant capable of identifying unusual activity, highlighting risky actions, and helping users understand exactly what they're approving.
